Jailbreak for 64-bit 10.x devices.

Only 64-bit devices with headphone jack supported. (A7-A9)

Installation

  1. Get the IPA.
  2. Take a shower.
  3. Prepare offerings.
  4. Provide your Apple ID, Mother's Maiden Name, and SSN.
  5. Do some stuff with Cydia Impactor.
  6. Press the only button in the app.
  7. Wait until it's done.
  8. Profit.
